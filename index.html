Texas

This is a picture of one of the many geographical regions of Texas, where I live. Texas, also known as the Lone Star State, is the southernmost state of the United States of America. It's bordered by New Mexico to the west, Oklahoma to the north, Arkansas to the northeast, Louisiana to the east, and by the country of Mexico to the south. There's also the Gulf of Mexico to the southeast. Because of it's location, Texas inherits a rich diveristy of culture, everything from English, to Hispanic, to African, to Native American. Texas is also known for having the biggest state fair in the country, lasting 24 days and honing in around 2.2 million participants annualy. The weather here depends on the region, but I find that the people are perhaps the nicest in the world. Texas is also the home to many big corporations like ExxonMobil, AT&T, Dell Technologies, and the owner of Dr Pepper. There's also H-E-B, which in my opinion is the best state-wide supermarket chain in the country. The Texas State Animals include the Nine-banded Armadillo, the semi-feral Texas Longhorn, the Mexican Free-tailed Bat, the Guadalupe Bass, the Monarch Butterfly, the Horny Toad, and the Lightning Whelk, .All in all, Texas to me seems like a great place to live.
